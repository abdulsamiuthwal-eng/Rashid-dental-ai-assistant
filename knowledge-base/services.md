# Rashid Dental Clinic — Services

> **Knowledge Base Status:** This document contains verified clinic information for the Rashid Dental AI Assistant.

---

## Overview of Services

Rashid Dental Clinic offers a comprehensive range of dental services for patients of all ages. From routine preventive care to advanced restorative and cosmetic procedures, our team is equipped to handle all your dental needs under one roof.

---

## General Dentistry

### Routine Check-Up and Examination
A comprehensive dental examination to assess the overall health of your teeth, gums, and mouth. Includes oral cancer screening and personalized care recommendations.

### Professional Teeth Cleaning (Prophylaxis)
Professional cleaning to remove plaque and tartar build-up that cannot be removed by regular brushing. Helps prevent cavities, gum disease, and bad breath.

### Dental X-Rays
Digital X-rays to detect issues not visible to the naked eye, including decay between teeth, bone loss, and infections. Low-radiation digital technology used.

### Tooth Fillings
Restoration of teeth damaged by decay using tooth-colored composite materials. Natural-looking and durable.

### Tooth Extractions
Removal of damaged, decayed, or impacted teeth. Performed under local anesthesia for a comfortable experience.

---

## Cosmetic Dentistry

### Teeth Whitening
Professional in-clinic whitening treatment to brighten and restore the natural colour of teeth. Results visible in a single session.

### Dental Veneers
Thin porcelain shells bonded to the front surface of teeth to improve appearance. Ideal for discolored, chipped, or misaligned teeth.

### Composite Bonding
Application of tooth-coloured resin to repair chips, cracks, or gaps in teeth. Completed in a single visit.

---

## Restorative Dentistry

### Dental Crowns
Custom-made caps placed over damaged teeth to restore their shape, strength, and appearance. Available in porcelain, ceramic, and metal options.

### Dental Bridges
Fixed prosthetic devices used to replace one or more missing teeth. Supported by adjacent natural teeth or implants.

### Dental Implants
Permanent titanium posts surgically placed into the jawbone to support artificial teeth. The gold standard for tooth replacement.

### Dentures
Removable replacements for missing teeth and surrounding tissues. Available in full and partial options.

---

## Orthodontics

### Traditional Braces
Metal or ceramic brackets and wires used to straighten teeth and correct bite issues. Suitable for patients of all ages.

### Clear Aligners
Removable, transparent trays as an alternative to traditional braces. Nearly invisible and comfortable.

---

## Periodontal (Gum) Treatment

### Scaling and Root Planing
Deep cleaning procedure to treat gum disease by removing plaque and tartar below the gum line.

### Gum Disease Management
Ongoing care and maintenance for patients with periodontal disease to prevent progression.

---

## Paediatric Dentistry

### Children's Dental Services
Dental care tailored to the needs of children, including:
- Preventive check-ups and cleaning
- Fluoride treatments
- Dental sealants
- Fillings for baby teeth
- Habit counselling (thumb sucking, pacifier use)

---

## Emergency Dental Care

Emergency appointments are available for urgent dental situations including:
- Severe tooth pain
- Knocked-out teeth
- Cracked or broken teeth
- Dental abscess
- Uncontrolled bleeding

Please call the clinic immediately for emergency cases. See the [Emergency Guidance](./emergency-guidance.md) document for details.

---

## Important Notice

The AI assistant can provide general information about dental procedures. However:

- It **cannot** recommend a specific treatment for your individual situation.
- It **cannot** diagnose dental conditions.
- It **cannot** confirm whether a specific service is currently available.
- Always consult your dentist for personalised treatment recommendations.
