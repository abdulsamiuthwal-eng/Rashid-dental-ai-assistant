# Rashid Dental Clinic — Appointments

> **Knowledge Base Status:** This document contains verified clinic information for the Rashid Dental AI Assistant.

---

## Booking an Appointment

Rashid Dental Clinic welcomes new and returning patients. Appointments can be requested through multiple channels for your convenience.

---

## How to Book

### Option 1: Via This AI Assistant
You can request an appointment directly through this chat. The assistant will collect the following information and forward your request to the clinic:

- **Your full name**
- **Phone number**
- **Preferred appointment date**
- **Preferred time of day** (morning / afternoon / evening)
- **Requested service** (check-up, cleaning, consultation, etc.)
- **Reason for visit**
- **Whether you are a new or returning patient**

> **Important:** Submitting an appointment request through the AI assistant is a **request only** — it is **not a confirmed booking**. The clinic will contact you to confirm your appointment time.

### Option 2: By Phone
Call us at **+1 (555) 123-4567** during opening hours to speak with our reception team.

### Option 3: By WhatsApp
Send a message to **+1 (555) 123-4567** with your name, preferred date and time, and reason for visit.

### Option 4: By Email
Send your booking request to **info@rashiddental.com** with your details and preferred appointment time.

### Option 5: Walk-in
Walk-in patients are welcome subject to availability. Please note that wait times may be longer for walk-in appointments.

---

## What to Bring to Your Appointment

For your first visit or any appointment, please bring:

- Valid photo identification
- Insurance card (if applicable)
- List of current medications
- Previous dental records (if available)
- Referral letter (if referred by another dentist)
- Any questions or concerns you'd like to discuss

---

## Cancellation Policy

We kindly ask that you provide at least **24 hours' notice** if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. This allows us to offer the appointment slot to another patient in need. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee.

---

## Appointment Duration

Appointment durations vary depending on the type of treatment:

| Appointment Type | Approximate Duration |
|-----------------|---------------------|
| Routine check-up | 30–45 minutes |
| Professional cleaning | 45–60 minutes |
| Simple filling | 30–60 minutes |
| Consultation | 20–30 minutes |
| Complex procedure | Varies — clinic will advise |

---

## New Patients

We welcome new patients at Rashid Dental Clinic. Your first visit typically includes:

1. Completion of patient registration form
2. Comprehensive dental examination
3. Digital X-rays (if needed)
4. Professional cleaning
5. Personalised treatment plan discussion

Please arrive 10-15 minutes early for your first appointment to complete the registration process.

---

## Emergency Appointments

For dental emergencies, please call the clinic immediately at **+1 (555) 123-4567** or our emergency line **+1 (555) 987-6543**. Please refer to the [Emergency Guidance](./emergency-guidance.md) document for more information.

---

## Important Notice

The AI assistant **cannot**:
- Confirm an appointment booking
- Access the clinic's appointment calendar
- Guarantee appointment availability on any specific date or time

The assistant can only collect your appointment request and forward it. The clinic will contact you to confirm.
