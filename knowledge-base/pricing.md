## Important Pricing Disclaimer

Dental pricing varies significantly based on:
- Individual patient assessment and treatment needs
- Materials used
- Complexity of the procedure
- Dentist's recommendation following examination

**The AI assistant cannot provide a price quote for your specific situation.** Pricing should always be confirmed with the clinic directly, following a consultation.
