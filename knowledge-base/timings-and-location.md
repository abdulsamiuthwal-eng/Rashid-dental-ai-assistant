## How to Get There

### By Car
The clinic is located on Healthcare Avenue, easily accessible from the main ring road. Free parking is available for patients in the clinic's private parking lot.

### By Public Transport
- **Bus:** Routes 12, 15, and 22 stop at "City Park" — a 2-minute walk from the clinic.
- **Metro:** The nearest metro station is "Medical District" (5-minute walk).

### Parking
Free parking is available for all patients. The parking lot has designated spaces for wheelchair users.
